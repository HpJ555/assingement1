The Alchemist

The Alchemist, a novel by Paulo Coelho, follows Santiago, an Andalusian shepherd boy, as he travels from Spain to the Egyptian desert in search of a treasure buried at the pyramids. This story, celebrated for its simple yet profound message, encourages readers to follow their dreams and listen to their hearts, ultimately finding their "Personal Legend". Along the way, Santiago encounters a Gypsy woman, a king, and an alchemist who guide him on his journey of self-discovery, leading him to realize that true treasure lies not just in material wealth, but in the experience and wisdom gained along the way.
