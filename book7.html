Pride and Prejudice

"Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen is a romantic comedy that follows the relationship between Elizabeth Bennet and Fitzwilliam Darcy, who are initially prejudiced against each other due to social differences and personal biases. Through their journey, they must overcome these prejudices to develop a deeper understanding of each other's character and eventually fall in love. The novel explores themes of social class, reputation, and the importance of individual character.

Published Date:28 January 1813

Author: Jane Austen
