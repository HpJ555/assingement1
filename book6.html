A Tale of Two Cities

"A Tale of Two Cities" is Charles Dickens's historical novel set in London and Paris before and during the French Revolution. The story follows the lives of Dr. Manette, his daughter Lucie, and Charles Darnay, the nephew of a marquis, as they navigate the upheaval and social unrest of the time. The novel explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the power of individuals to make a difference in the face of historical events.

Published Date: 26 November 1859

Author: Charles Dickens
