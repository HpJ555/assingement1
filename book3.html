The Lord of the Rings

In a sleepy village in the Shire, young Frodo Baggins finds himself faced with an immense task, as the Ring is entrusted to his care. He must leave his home and make a perilous journey across the realms of Middle-earth to the Crack of Doom, deep inside the territories of the Dark Lord..

Published Date: 29 July 1954

Author:John Ronald Reuel Tolkien
