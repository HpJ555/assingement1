How to Bake a Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps to Bake a Cake

  1. Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. Take a bowl
  3. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time to the butter mixture, beating well after each addition.
  6. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pa n.

  10. Let the cake cool before serving or frosting.
