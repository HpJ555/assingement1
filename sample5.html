Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It's a world-renowned landmark and a symbol of Paris, known for its iconic design and historical significance. The tower was built for the 1889 World's Fair (Exposition Universelle) to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution.

Notre Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral, a renowned Gothic masterpiece, is a prominent cathedral church located in Paris, France, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Construction began in the 12th century and continued over several decades, incorporating various Gothic architectural styles. It is celebrated for its grandeur, intricate details, and its role in significant historical and religious events.
